Title Author Summary
Wizard of Earthsea Ursula Le Guin Young and proud, Ged the wizard performs magics beyond his ability and releases a unknown evil into the world. The book follows Ged's origin, training, and eventually his quest to find and defeat the evil of his own creation.
Caves of Steel Issac Asimov In a future, subterranean earth, a robot commits murder. This should be impossible. The laws of robitics built in to every robot ever made prevent them from harming humans. Detective Elijah Bailey and R. Daneel are assigned to the case.
