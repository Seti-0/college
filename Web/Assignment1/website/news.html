4.0 Release!

I am happy to annouce the latest release! After a long, long beta.

This milestone sees a major rework of the package system, introducing a new in-editor marketplace to ease sharing of plugins. Other goals included performance and reducing object allocation in the main rendering loop, improvements to coroutines, and a new GLSL editor.

See the wiki for a full list of improvements!
