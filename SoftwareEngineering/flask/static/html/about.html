About this site

View current station and stand availability

You can use this page to view current bike or stand availability by using the toggle button at the bottom of the page. This is based on real-time information from the bike stations and is updated every five minutes. A traffic light system is used to indicate the relative availability of bikes or stands, as appropriate, at each station for either the current time, or future predictions. Red indicates 20% or less availability, orange 30% or less, and green indicates above 30% availability.

View information about stations

Clicking on a station marker will show you more information about the station, including the name/address, and whether the station accepts card payment or not.

View charts of average past availability

In the bottom left corner, there is the option to display charts for the currently selected station. With this you can see the average past availability for the station for that day of the week, and how it compares for the current dayâ€™s availability.

View predicted availability

You can also enter a time and date within the next five days to view our predictions for availability. The prediction model uses historical occupancy and weather data, combined with the weather forecast for the requested day/time to predict the bike/stand availability of each station.

Search for nearby stations

If you enter a location in the search box, the map will zoom to show you nearby stations and the availability at each. The closest station to your location will be highlighted in purple.

Plan a route

The app also offers a route planner, which allows you to enter a start location and an end location, finds the nearest bike station to each and shows you the cycling route between them.
